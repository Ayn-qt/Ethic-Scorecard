About the AI Ethics Scoring System

The AI Ethics Scoring System is a project aimed at bridging modern technology with the diverse moral perspectives found in the world's major religions. In today's rapidly advancing AI landscape, it's essential to embed ethical checks that reflect a broad range of cultural and spiritual beliefs.

This system takes user-submitted ethical dilemmas and evaluates them using principles derived from Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Buddhism, Sikhism, and other major worldviews. It generates a score along with reasoning based on each tradition's teachings.

The purpose is not to compare religions, but to offer inclusive perspectives that help developers, researchers, and policymakers reflect on ethical dimensions more comprehensively.

We envision this system helping build AI that is not only efficient but also empathetic and value-conscious.
